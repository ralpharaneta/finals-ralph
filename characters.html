YUJI ITADORI

Yuji Itadori is a compassionate and physically gifted teenager who, after swallowing a cursed object, becomes entangled in the dangerous world of jujutsu sorcery, wielding immense power while struggling with the curse of the malevolent spirit Sukuna inside him.

MEGUMI FUSHIGURO

Megumi Fushiguro is a calm and skilled jujutsu sorcerer with a strong sense of justice, specializing in summoning powerful Shikigami, while grappling with his complicated family background and personal ideals.

NOBARA KUGASAKI

Nobara Kugisaki is a confident and determined jujutsu sorcerer with a bold personality, known for her fierce combat style using cursed tools, and she quickly becomes one of Yuji and Megumiâ€™s closest allies at Jujutsu High.

MAKI ZENIN

Maki Zenin is a fierce and determined jujutsu sorcerer, known for her exceptional combat skills and mastery of cursed tools, despite being born without the ability to use cursed energy, which drives her to prove herself within the Zenin family and the jujutsu world.

TOGE INUNAKI

Toge Inumaki is a quiet and reserved jujutsu sorcerer with the unique ability to use cursed speech, where his words carry powerful curses, making him communicate primarily through rice ball ingredients to avoid accidental harm to others.

PANDA

Panda is a unique jujutsu sorcerer who is a cursed corpse with a humanoid form, possessing remarkable strength, a vibrant personality, and loyalty to his friends at Jujutsu High.

SATORU GOJO

Satoru Gojo is an incredibly powerful and charismatic jujutsu sorcerer, known for his mastery of the Limitless and Six Eyes techniques, and his confident, carefree attitude as a mentor at Jujutsu High.

ATSUYA KUSAKABE

Kusakabe is a jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at Jujutsu High, known for his calm demeanor and his role in training students, particularly in the use of cursed techniques.

SHOKO IEIRI

Shoko Ieiri is a skilled jujutsu sorcerer and a medical expert at Jujutsu High, known for her calm and laid-back personality, as well as her ability to heal injuries using cursed techniques.

SUGURU GETO

Suguru Geto is a former jujutsu sorcerer turned antagonist, once a close friend of Satoru Gojo, who becomes disillusioned with the jujutsu society and seeks to eliminate non-sorcerers in his quest to create a world ruled by curses.

AOI TODO

Aoi Todo is a confident and powerful jujutsu sorcerer known for his immense strength, unorthodox fighting style, and his enthusiastic, straightforward personality, often forming a close bond with Yuji Itadori after their first encounter.

NORITOSHI KAMO

Noritoshi Kamo is a jujutsu sorcerer from the influential Kamo family, known for his mastery of the Blood Manipulation technique, and for his cold, calculating personality and ambition within the jujutsu world.

MOMO NISHIYA

Momo Nishimiya is a jujutsu sorcerer and student at Jujutsu High, known for her exceptional skills in manipulating cursed energy and her friendly, supportive personality, especially toward her peers.

MAI ZENIN

Mai Zenin is a jujutsu sorcerer and the twin sister of Maki Zenin, known for her mastery of cursed tools and her more reserved, often cynical personality, as well as her struggle with living in the shadow of her more powerful sister.

MASUMI MIWA

Miwa Kasumi is a jujutsu sorcerer and student at Jujutsu High, known for her determination and strong sense of justice, though she is often portrayed as inexperienced compared to other students, relying on her sword skills in battle.

ULTIMATE MECH

Mechamaru is a powerful, remote-controlled automaton that Kokichi Muta uses to fight in jujutsu battles, as Muta himself has a debilitating condition that confines him to a weak, bedridden body.

KOKICHI MUTA

Kokichi Muta is a jujutsu sorcerer who controls the mechanical body *Mechamaru* due to his own frail condition, using it to fight and carry out jujutsu tasks while harboring feelings of resentment and conflict.

UTAHIME IORI

Utahime Iori is a jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at Kyoto Jujutsu High, known for her calm and composed personality, as well as her proficiency in cursed techniques, particularly in her role as a mentor to students like Maki and Mai Zenin.

YOSHINOBU GAKUKANJI

Gakuganji is a jujutsu sorcerer and the head of Kyoto Jujutsu High, known for his authoritative and traditional views on the jujutsu world, often clashing with Satoru Gojo and his more progressive ideals.

KENTO NANAMI

Kento Nanami is a skilled and pragmatic jujutsu sorcerer, known for his calm demeanor, strong sense of responsibility, and mastery of the "Ratio Technique," which he uses to efficiently defeat curses.

TAKUMA INO

Takuma Ino is a jujutsu sorcerer at the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College, known for his laid-back personality and ability to control insects in combat.

MEI MEI

Mei Mei is a skilled and confident jujutsu sorcerer from the Kyoto branch, known for her powerful cursed technique that allows her to manipulate crows for reconnaissance and combat.

UI UI

Ui Ui is an apprentice of Mei Mei, known for his calm demeanor and skill in cursed techniques, though he plays a minor role in the story.

NAOBITO ZENIN

Mobito Zenin is a member of the Zenin family, known for his calm and serious demeanor, as well as his powerful cursed technique, which involves manipulating the cursed energy flow.

YUKI TSUKUMO

Yuki Tsukumo is a powerful and mysterious jujutsu sorcerer, known for her immense strength and unique cursed technique that allows her to manipulate and control the powerful curse of a mass of cursed energy.

MAHITO

Mahito is a malevolent and dangerous cursed spirit in the series, known for his ability to manipulate souls and reshape them, making him a formidable opponent.

JOGO

Jogo is a powerful cursed spirit in the series, known for his volcanic, fiery appearance and his ability to control and manipulate fire, making him a dangerous adversary.

HANAMI

Hanami is a mysterious and powerful cursed spirit, known for their deep connection to nature and ability to manipulate plants and trees for combat.

DAGON

Dagon is a high-ranking cursed spirit in the series, known for his aquatic abilities and control over water, which he uses to create deadly illusions and devastating attacks.

CHOSO

Choso is a cursed spirit with a human-like appearance, known for his unique blood manipulation abilities and his complex, emotional connection to his family and the events surrounding his creation.

HARUTA SHIGUREMO

Haruta is a student from the Kyoto school, known for his calm demeanor and combat skills, and is involved in the events during the Shibuya Incident.

TOJI FUSHIGURO

Toji Fushiguro is a former jujutsu sorcerer and skilled assassin, known for his exceptional physical abilities, lack of cursed energy, and mastery of cursed tools.

URAUME

Uraume is a mysterious and powerful figure, often associated with cursed spirits, known for their icy abilities and close connection to Sukuna.

RYOMEN SUKUNA

Sukuna is an ancient and immensely powerful curse, once a human sorcerer, known for his brutal nature, mastery of cursed techniques, and overwhelming strength that makes him a formidable threat to all.
